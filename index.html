Welcome to Casa Papelillo

Bold, open, organic —
deeply connected

Nestled on the cliffs of Chamela Bay — within the exclusive Costa Careyes community — this villa offers uninterrupted 180-degree ocean views and an intimate connection with the raw beauty of the Pacific.

Its design merges local craftsmanship with understated elegance: hand-polished stucco walls, natural stone, and thatched palm palapas that rise like living sculptures — a space where art, nature, and time coexist.
